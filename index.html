About Me

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I am under the supervision of Prof. Zhiwei Xiong and Prof. Dong Liu. I obtained my B. Eng. degree in the Department of Automation (Lang Shijun Honors Class) from Northeastern University (NEU, China) in 2018.

My research interests broadly lie in the areas of computational photography, computational imaging, and deep learning (e.g., super-resolution, denoising, enhancement, high-dynamic-range imaging, and hyper-spectral image processing).

I will join The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) as a Postdoctoral Researcher, advised by Prof. Jinwei Gu.
